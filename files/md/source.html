Update on the Economic Stabilization Act:

The Economic Stabilization Act was enacted under Article II, § 17(c) of the Maryland Constitution on May 30, 2021 and the Department of Labor is actively working to develop regulations that facilitate implementation. Should you have an interest in this topic, we encourage you to check the Maryland Register Online or contact our Department’s Dislocation Services Unit at sheila.bouloubassis@maryland.gov.
